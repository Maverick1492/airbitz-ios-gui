Tranzaksyon Lis

Chak tranzaksyon montre se yon valè wouj oswa vèt ki se kantite lajan ou te depanse oswa ou te resevwa nan bitcoin ak fiat (USD, CAD, EUR, elatriye).

Tap the top left button (Balance) to toggle viewing your running balance at each transaction.

Tap the total balance at the top to toggle between showing your balance or hiding it from view.

Search your transactions by dragging down on the transactions list and using the Search field. Enter a Payee, Category, or text from a Notes field to search.

Tap the wallet name header at the top to list your other wallets, create a new wallet, rename a wallet, delete a wallet, export your transactions, or to select another wallet for use.
